An Atlantic archipelago of barely 525,000 people, the Blue Sharks are the smallest nation by population ever to reach a World Cup knockout stage — a fairytale debut. Coached since 2020 by Bubista and captained by all-time great Ryan Mendes, they drew with Spain, Uruguay and Saudi Arabia to finish second in Group H and set up a Round of 32 date with reigning champions Argentina.
